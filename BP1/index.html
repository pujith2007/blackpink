this is BLACKPINK

BLACKPINK is a world-renowned South Korean girl group formed by YG Entertainment in 2016, consisting of Jisoo, Jennie, Rosé, and Lisa. Known for their powerful performances, stunning visuals, and chart-topping songs, they have become global icons of K-pop. With hits like Ddu-Du Ddu-Du, Kill This Love, and How You Like That, BLACKPINK has broken numerous records and built one of the largest fanbases worldwide, known as BLINKs. Beyond music, the members are trendsetters in fashion and culture, representing top brands and influencing millions across the globe. Their success reflects not only talent but also their role as pioneers bringing K-pop to international stages.
